Partner Dashboard
Partner Portal Desktop View For Campbell + Mike June 2026
The partner-facing view inside the Stones app. Partners log in here to see how their content is performing, track earnings, engage with community discussions in their area, and manage their profile. Data and layout is indicative — all numbers are mocked.
Remuneration model

Quarterly payouts. Minimum $100 threshold. Earnings below threshold carry forward to the next quarter automatically. Premium Partners qualify by delivering 2+ content pieces per year.

Community surfacing

On login, the dashboard surfaces 2–3 live discussions in the partner's declared expertise areas. Partners can respond directly, driving visibility and reinforcing their position in the ecosystem.

Profile management

Partners can update their expertise tags and submit new content pieces from within the dashboard. Changes to expertise areas affect how community discussions are surfaced to them.

Design notes for Campbell + Mike — Sidebar uses brand dark green (#2C3E2F), not the app's standard dark. The partner portal is visually distinct from the leader-facing experience but shares the same type system and colour tokens. All data shown is mocked — actual figures will pull from the platform's analytics layer. The qualification bar at the top should only appear when the partner has delivered both required content pieces; replace with a progress indicator ("1 of 2 pieces delivered") when they haven't. The $100 threshold and carry-forward logic should be configurable in the admin layer. Earnings figures shown in NZD by default; currency should respect the partner's registered country.
