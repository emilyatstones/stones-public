Partner Profile — Leader View
In-App Page Leader-Facing Linked from Directory June 2026
Shown when a leader taps "View Profile" from the directory. Gives a richer picture of who the partner is, what they've contributed to Stones, and how to reach them. No earnings data is visible here — this is a leader experience.
Content cards

Each card links to the live piece of Stones content — Practice opens in the Practices flow, Story opens inline. Leaders can start or continue the content directly from this page.

Engagement counts

The leader-visible stat is completions and views — not earnings. Frames the partner as high-impact and well-regarded without surfacing anything commercial.

Enquiry path

LinkedIn and website open in browser. No in-app message flow — Stones connects the dots; the relationship is the partner's to close. Consider adding an "Enquire" mailto link at a later stage.

About Sarah

Sarah Mitchell has spent 15 years helping leaders and teams communicate with clarity and confidence in the moments that matter most. Her work sits at the intersection of communication science and practical leadership — drawing on research in influence, trust, and interpersonal psychology to help leaders navigate the conversations they've been avoiding, resolve disagreement without damaging relationships, and build the kind of candour that makes teams genuinely effective.

Before moving into coaching and facilitation, Sarah spent a decade in senior communications roles across financial services and technology. That background gives her work a pragmatism her clients find refreshing: less theory, more language you can actually use on Monday morning.

She is a Professional Certified Coach (ICF-PCC) and a trained facilitator. She works with individual leaders through her private practice and with leadership teams across New Zealand and Australia.
