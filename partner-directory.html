Partner Directory — Leader View
In-App Page Leader-Facing For Campbell + Mike June 2026
The in-app partner index, visible to all leaders. Filterable by domain and subdomain. Each card shows the partner's expertise, a short bio, their Stones content count, and direct links to their LinkedIn and website. Partners do not appear here unless they have at least one published piece of content.
Filter logic

Domain tabs filter the subdomain chip row below. Selecting a subdomain chip filters the card grid. "All" shows every partner. Multiple subdomain chips can be selected simultaneously.

Card ordering

Default sort: most active content this quarter first. Secondary sort: alphabetical. Premium partners (active remuneration) shown with a green dot indicator. Leaders can sort by A–Z or content recency.

Enquiry flow

LinkedIn and website links open in browser. No in-app enquiry form — Stones surfaces the partner; the relationship is theirs to close. "View Profile" shows the partner's full bio and all their content.

Design notes for Campbell + Mike — This is the leader-facing view; it uses the standard Stones green accent (not the partner-portal blue). Domain colour coding is consistent across the app: Self = Willow/gold, People = Terracotta, Operational = Wave Blue, Strategic = Forest Green. The premium dot (small green circle, top-right of card) appears only for Premium Partners. Partners with no published content are not shown. "View Profile" leads to a full partner profile page (not mocked here) showing their complete bio, all Stones content, and contact links. Filter chips in the subdomain row should be dynamically generated from the 23 subdomains in the content architecture.
