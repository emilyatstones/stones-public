Stones is built for that gap. Not for the curated version of leadership — the real one. The Sunday night before a hard week. The conversation you have been rehearsing and still do not feel ready for. The moment you are not sure if what you are doing is working.

Your development foundation

Goals
Set your direction. See yourself move.
Set your development goals and break them into milestones. Progress is tracked over time so growth feels visible and real — not abstract or assumed. Connected to your Compass so your goals reflect what actually matters, not what sounds good.

Support in the moment
For when you need it, not when it is scheduled.
Practices
Help when you are in the middle of something hard.
Behavioural learning loops built for moments of need. Struggling with a specific situation — a performance conversation, managing self-doubt, a team in conflict? Enter a Practice. Expert video, a practical tool, a reflection prompt, and a follow-up nudge guide you through it. Not passive learning. A real loop of action and reflection.
You come out the other side with something to show for it — not just knowledge, but a skill you actually used.
Toolkits
Pick up and use today.
Templates, scripts, conversation frameworks, and practical guides. Not things to read — things to use. The script for a feedback conversation you have been avoiding. The planning template for a team session. The framework for a decision you have been turning over. Ready when you are.
You stop staring at a blank page. You start the conversation. You run the session.
Community
Ask real questions. Get real answers.
A peer forum where leaders ask the things they cannot ask inside their organisations and get honest responses from people who have been there. Alongside peers, Stones' expert partners are in the community — so you get both lived experience and specialist perspective, in the same place.
